Mission

About

At Adebouge Holdings, our goal is to make sure every high schooler can get a job after they have completed 3 years of secondary school education and be able to create jobs after they have completed 6 years. To accomplish this lofty goal, we are leveraging our unique partnerships with established strategic partners in the TechEd space and impact investors by connecting digital transformation to corporate philanthropy.

Adebouge Holdings seeks to channel Bayo Adebogun's experience implementing climate change related initiatives, enabling well architected and secured digital transformation in the cloud technology space and his ability to bring together diverse groups of like-minded global stakeholders to collaborate in the business of doing good through impact investment.

What we do

Digital transformation

Helping small businesses build sustainable and more secured digital infrastructure

Corporate Philantropy

Economically empowering underresourced communities through strategic partnership with established impact organizations to design and develop value education initiatives, encourage active citizenship and solve livelihood problems.
